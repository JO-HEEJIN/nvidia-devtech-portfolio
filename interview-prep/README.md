## Interview Preparation Strategy

### Interview Stages (Typical)
1. Phone Screen (30-45 min)
   - Recruiter or hiring manager
   - Background and motivation
   - Basic technical questions

2. Technical Interview (45-60 min)
   - Deep technical questions
   - Coding/problem-solving
   - System design (possibly)

3. Behavioral Interview (30-45 min)
   - STAR method questions
   - Cultural fit
   - Past experiences

4. Final Round (varies)
   - Team interviews
   - Presentation (possibly)
   - Q&A with team members

## Questions to Ask Interviewers

### About the Role
- What does a typical day look like for a DevTech intern?
- What projects would I be working on?
- What technologies/tools does the team use?
- How is success measured in this role?

### About the Team
- How is the DevTech team structured?
- What's the team culture like?
- How does the team collaborate?
- What opportunities are there for learning?

### About NVIDIA
- What excites you most about working at NVIDIA?
- How does NVIDIA support intern development?
- What are the biggest challenges facing the team?
- What's the path from intern to full-time?

### About Growth
- What learning opportunities are available?
- Are there mentorship programs?
- How does NVIDIA invest in employee development?
- What's the career progression path?

## Pre-Interview Checklist

### Day Before
- [ ] Review resume and cover letter
- [ ] Review job description
- [ ] Research interviewers on LinkedIn
- [ ] Prepare questions to ask
- [ ] Test video/audio setup (if virtual)
- [ ] Prepare professional outfit
- [ ] Get good night's sleep

### Day Of
- [ ] Review STAR stories
- [ ] Review technical concepts
- [ ] Arrive/login 10 minutes early
- [ ] Have resume and notes ready
- [ ] Have water nearby
- [ ] Silence phone
- [ ] Positive mindset

### After Interview
- [ ] Send thank-you email within 24 hours
- [ ] Note down questions asked
- [ ] Reflect on performance
- [ ] Identify areas for improvement
