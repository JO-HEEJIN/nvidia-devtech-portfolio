# Behavioral Interview Preparation

## STAR Method

Structure your answers using:
- Situation: Set the context
- Task: Describe your responsibility
- Action: Explain what you did
- Result: Share the outcome

---

## Common Questions

### Leadership & Teamwork
1. Tell me about a time you led a project
2. Describe a situation where you had to work with a difficult team member
3. How do you handle conflicts in a team?
4. Give an example of when you helped a teammate

### Problem Solving
1. Describe a challenging technical problem you solved
2. Tell me about a time you failed
3. How do you approach debugging complex issues?
4. Describe a time you had to learn a new technology quickly

### Communication
1. Explain a complex technical concept to a non-technical person
2. Describe a time you had to present your work
3. How do you handle feedback?
4. Tell me about a time you had to convince someone of your approach

### NVIDIA-Specific
1. Why NVIDIA?
2. Why DevTech specifically?
3. What excites you about GPU computing?
4. How do you stay updated with AI/GPU technologies?
5. Describe your experience with NVIDIA technologies

## Questions to Ask

### About the Role
- What does a typical day look like?
- What projects would I work on?
- How is success measured?
- What technologies does the team use?

### About the Team
- How is the team structured?
- What's the team culture like?
- How does the team collaborate?
- What opportunities for learning?

### About Growth
- What learning resources are available?
- Are there mentorship programs?
- What's the career progression path?
- How does NVIDIA invest in employee development?

---

## Tips

- Be specific with examples
- Quantify results when possible
- Show enthusiasm for NVIDIA and GPU technology
- Be honest about what you don't know
- Ask clarifying questions
- Think out loud during technical discussions
